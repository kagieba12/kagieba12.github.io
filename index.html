    • The top 5 places that you NEED to tick off on your bucket list

    Read Time: 10 minutes

    1. The Maldives

    Located in South Asia, the Maldives is the best tourist destination on the list. Activities range from water activities such as skydiving and snorkelling to going onto a relaxing cruise whilst the sun sets. Not only are the views absouletely stunning, there's a wide range of national dishes to try! Whether you're flying solo or with your family, Maldives is the ideal place to go!

    2. Dubai, UAE

    Dubai is well known for it's insanely impressive skyscrapers, with the Burj Khalifa being a must visit! In addition to the insane architecture, Dubai is full of shopping malls, beaches, 5 star hotels and has everything a tourist would ever need.

    3. Paris, France

    An absoulete classic. Located in the heart of Paris, is one of the world's most famous tourist attractions: the Eiffel Tower. In additon to this, there are several sites to see that can be ticked off your bucket list, including the Seine River, Disneyland Paris, the Louvre and many more.

    4. New York City, USA

    An American Dream. NYC is not only one of the busiest cities in the world, but it is one of the most popular tourist attractions, especially during christmas. Ranging from insanely impressive skylines to beautiful green landscapes such as Central Park, NYC has it all. Notable tourist attractions include the Empire State Building. Statue of Liberty, Central Park and Times Square.

    5. Venice, Italy

    If you are all for unique architectures and landscapes, then Venice is the one for you! Venice is well known for it's unique mode of transport: the Gondola! As Venice is very popular and is mostly situated within water, the best months to visit are typically from April to October.
